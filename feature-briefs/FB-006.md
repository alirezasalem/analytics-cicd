# FB-006: Track Payment Info Submission

## What & Why
When a customer submits their payment details during checkout, we need to capture what payment method they selected and where they are in the funnel. This helps us understand payment method preferences and identify drop-off at the payment step.

This event should fire once when the user clicks "Continue" or "Next" after selecting or entering their payment method on the checkout payment step.

## When does it fire?
User submits payment information on the checkout payment step (`/checkout/payment`).

## What do we need to capture?

- Total order value at the time of payment submission
- Currency
- Coupon code applied (if any)
- Payment method selected (e.g. credit card, PayPal, bank transfer)
- All products currently in the cart — name, ID, price, quantity, category

## Important notes for the dev team
- This event fires on submission of the payment step, not on final order confirmation
- If the user goes back and changes their payment method, the event should fire again with the new method
- Coupon field should be empty string if no coupon was applied
- Do not include any raw card numbers or sensitive payment data — only the payment type label

## Out of scope
- Payment validation errors
- Failed payment attempts
- The final purchase confirmation (that's a separate purchase event)
